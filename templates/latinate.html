Latinate words (words with romance etymology) aren't inherently bad. But they often sound pretentious or snooty. Replacing them with germanic words can strengthen your prose. Why say chamber when you can say room? Why write irate when you can say angry or mad? This tool helps track down latinate words you might replace with germanic counterparts. The world is full of latinate words. We track about 300 of them (see the list on the right of this page).
